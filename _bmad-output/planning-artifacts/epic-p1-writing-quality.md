# Epic P1 — Writing Quality

**Priority:** P1  
**Status:** Ready for refinement and phased execution  
**Objective:** Make GHOSTWRITR produce books that feel authored, coherent, and category-appropriate.

## Why this epic exists

The platform can already move books through meaningful stages. The next gap is not structure, but output quality:

- nonfiction drafts need stronger synthesis
- fiction drafts need stronger scene memory and continuity
- the editor agent needs to reason at both book and chapter level
- stage UX should support high-quality author decisions rather than exposing internal machinery

## Stories

1. `P1.1` Deepen nonfiction draft intelligence
2. `P1.2` Deepen fiction draft intelligence
3. `P1.3` Expand the editor agent
4. `P1.4` Polish stage UX

## Exit criteria

- nonfiction chapters feel woven rather than assembled
- fiction chapters hold continuity and scene causality more reliably
- Editing behaves like a strong ghostwriting/editorial partner
- product surfaces feel intentional and guidance-rich
